Frequently Asked Question

+ - How do I start the process of hiring a foreign worker through Workvantage recruitment and staffing agency?

To start the process, contact us for a consultation. We’ll discuss your specific needs and guide you through each step, from job postings to candidate selection and documentation.

+ - What industries do you specialize in for recruitment?

We specialize in various industries, including Greenhouse, Food Processing, Construction & Trades, Manufacturing & Warehousing, fish processing, Hotel & Restaurants and more. Our diverse network allows us to find the best talent for your specific industry requirements.

+ - Can you help with both temporary and permanent staffing needs?

Yes, we provide solutions for both temporary and permanent staffing needs. Whether you need short-term project workers or long-term employees, we’ve got you covered.

+ - How long does the recruitment process take?

The whole process (Recruitment, LMIA, Visa and Travel arrangement) takes between 3 to 6 months, depending on the complexity of the position and the specific requirements of the job. We strive to make the process as efficient as possible.

+ - What support do you offer after the hiring process is complete?

We offer immigration ongoing support, including assistance with work permit renewals and compliance with immigration regulations. Our goal is to ensure a smooth transition and continued success.

+ - What are the benefits of working with Workvantage compared to other agencies?

As the leading staffing agency in Canada, Workvantage offers personalized service, extensive industry expertise, and a proven track record of successful placements. We handle all the legal and administrative complexities, providing a hassle-free experience for employers.

+ - What is the LMIA process and how does it work?

The LMIA process involves obtaining permission from Employment and Social Development Canada (ESDC) to hire a foreign worker. We assist with every step, ensuring all necessary documentation is prepared and submitted accurately.

+ - What types of work permits do you handle?

We handle various types of work permits, including employer-specific permits, open work permits, and permits under the International Mobility Program. We tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

+ - Can you assist with permanent residence applications?

Yes, we provide support for permanent residence applications, helping your employees secure their long-term future in Canada. We guide you through the entire process to ensure success.

+ - What are the costs associated with your services?

The costs vary depending on the specific services required and the complexity of each case. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your needs. We strive to offer competitive and transparent pricing.
